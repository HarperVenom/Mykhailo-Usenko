PROJECTS

CinemaRM

Interactive movie roadmaps. For people who love to dive deep into fictional worlds and fully explore them.

The Idea

As a lover of movies and big cinematic universes, I came up with the idea of collecting all the titles on a single page—not as a simple list but in a much more immersive and entertaining way—to make it easy for people to navigate and explore, without constantly searching the internet and trying to figure out connections between titles.

Features

- I visualized a map of all the titles in the form of a tree so people could see how each title is connected to another.

- I let users filter the titles by branches, where each branch tells a story about diffrent heroes and rebuild the map based on the fiilters. This way, users could customize their experience.

- I also let users mark each title as completed and save it on their account so they could track their progress and continue from where they stopped last time.

Challenges

It was a real challenge to figure out the way to create a tree of all titles based on a single data field. I've gone through several attempts to create the algorithm for placing each title in a proper spot to avoid making it look messy. I ended up separating all the titles in columns by the amount of parent layers and filling intermidiate levels without titles with empty blocks that connect two trails.

Adding the zoom feature also turned out very difficult for me. I faced a lot of bugs trying to implement it so that map wouldn't shift on scale change.

Issues

Though all the listed challenges are partially overcome, the result is still not as good as I wanted it to be. The map may still look unorganized in some cases, and the zoom feature is not very flexible.

What I learnt

I spent a lot of time working with React states and components, which improved my understanding of the concepts. I also worked with backend and created my own API, which helped me understand this side of development better.
